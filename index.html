Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

In progressive enhancement, it focuses on content. Allowing everyone to at least have access to the basic content and function of a web page. Also letting the people with extra features such as fast internet to have even better versions instead. Progressive enhancement is useful in the modern web since it works for all types of users and their needs, making it accessible for everyone. It provides the essential content and functionality to as many users. While also giving better experience, to other users who are able to. So every user can see the most important thing which is the content in this case. It is important since it ensures stability across the browsers.
